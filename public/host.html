Movement
  • Roll the dice on your turn. Must roll a 6 to leave home base.
  • Tokens move clockwise around the 52-square outer track.
  • Rolling a 6 grants an extra roll after moving.
  • Three consecutive 6s = FOUL – turn is forfeited.
Capture
  • Landing on an opponent sends them back to home. Grants a bonus roll.
  • START and STOP squares are safe – no captures there.
Finishing

  • First to finish all 4 tokens wins.
